🎉 **Exciting News!**

I am thrilled to share that I have been awarded an **ELIAS Mobility Grant** for a research visit to work with **Professor [Marta Kwiatkowska](https://www.cs.ox.ac.uk/people/marta.kwiatkowska/)** in the **Department of Computer Science at the University of Oxford** from **May to June 2026**!  

This visit supports the goals of the [**ELIAS European Lighthouse of AI for Sustainability**](https://elias-ai.eu) by advancing **automated verification methods for LLM-based robotics**, thereby promoting both **sustainability** and **responsible AI**.  

Our collaboration aims to contribute to more **transparent, accountable, and trustworthy AI-driven decision-making**, while reinforcing the **reliability**, and **ethical integrity** emphasised by ELIAS network.
