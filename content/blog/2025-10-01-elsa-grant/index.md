ðŸŽ‰ **Exciting News!**

I am thrilled to share that I have been awarded an **ELSA Mobility Grant** for a research visit to work with **Professor [Marta Kwiatkowska](https://www.cs.ox.ac.uk/people/marta.kwiatkowska/)** in the **Department of Computer Science at the University of Oxford** from **October to December 2025**!  

This visit supports the goals of the [**ELSA European Lighthouse on Secure and Safe AI**](https://elsa-ai.eu) by advancing **automated verification methods for LLM-based robotics**, thereby promoting both **trustworthy** and **responsible AI**.  

Our collaboration aims to enable more **transparent, accountable, and trustworthy AI-driven decision-making**, while reinforcing the **reliability** upheld by the ELSA network.
